What We Offer

Everything Paint — One Store

From automotive finishes to house paint and stain, we stock the full PPG lineup and the equipment to apply it.

🚗

Automotive Paint & Finishes

Full PPG automotive systems in stock — factory OEM color codes, basecoat/clearcoat, single-stage, primers, sealers, and reducers. Everything a body shop needs, ready to go.

🎨

Color Matching

Bring us any physical sample and our experienced staff will match it by eye using PPG's tinting system. We also make custom automotive touch-up bottles — perfect for covering scratches and chips on your vehicle. Custom matches are ready within 24 hours.

🥫

Custom Spray Cans & Touch-Up Bottles

We fill custom-matched aerosol spray cans and touch-up bottles in any color and finish. Touch-up bottles are ideal for car paint scratches and chips. Spray cans work great for small repairs, trim, and hard-to-reach areas.

🔫

Spray Equipment

We sell HVLP spray guns for precision automotive and finish work, plus air assisted airless sprayers built for house paint and large surface coverage. Whether you're spraying a car panel or a house exterior, we have the right gun.

🏠

House Paint & Stain

Interior and exterior PPG formulations in every sheen — flat through gloss. We also carry a full range of stains for decks, fences, and siding. Ask about our air assisted airless sprayers for fast, professional coverage on large projects.

🛡️

Supplies & Safety Gear

Everything you need beyond the paint — brushes, rollers, masking tape, drop cloths, sandpaper, body fillers, prep products, and protective safety gear including respirators, gloves, and eye protection.
